CALL FOR ENTRIES
Deadlines November 28, 2008 and January 2, 2009

Sans Souci, an international festival of dance cinema, screens short works that integrate dance with cinematic elements. We have an expansive definition of dance and an appreciation for highly experimental and interdisciplinary forms, including mixed-media works that incorporate live performance.

We invite submissions from all artists regardless of credentials and affiliations. Literally meaning “no worries” in French, Sans Souci hopes to attract both emerging and established dance-video and dance-film artists. We maintain reasonable entry fees of $25 and $40 for the early and final deadlines respectively.

Accepted works will be exhibited at our Sixth Annual Sans Souci Festival to be held in Boulder, Colorado, USA on Friday and Saturday, March 20 and 21, 2009, at the Dairy Center for the Arts. Selected works may also be invited to tour in the USA and at international venues.

“With its expansive definition of dance and its appreciation for highly experimental and interdisciplinary forms, Sans Souci is growing into an important festival,” says Nancy Spanier, internationally recognized choreographer and videographer.
