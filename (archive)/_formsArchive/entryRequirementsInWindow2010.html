Requirements
1.  With each entry submitted, include:
  • media (see item 3 below),
  • the appropriate entry fee (unless you pay in advance via PayPal — see item 2 below),
  • the printed Entry Form (select "Print" on the Online Entry Form), and
  • production photos or screen shots (see item 4 below).
Submissions for works that incorporate a live performance in conjunction with the screening must also include video of the live performance for evaluation.
2.  Deadlines & Payment. Your submission must arrive at our address before midnight on the deadline date.

3.  Media. Submitted media must be mini-DV or DVD (viewable in RCE Region 1, US and Canada). All media must be in NTSC format — unfortunately, we cannot support PAL or SECAM.

Please clearly label all media and media containers with the title of the work and your name.
4.  PR Images. Digital images are strongly preferred but printed images will be accepted. Digital images should be submitted at both 72 dpi AND 300 dpi. The digital JPEGs, PNGs, or GIFs may be submitted on CD or DVD or they may be specified by a URL which links directly to the image. Please include photo credit information for production photos or other images that are not screen captures.

Further Information
  • Acceptance Criteria. We're interested in work that integrates dance and cinematography. Works may include live dance performance in a mixed-media context with dance cinema. When choosing works, we consider production values, thoughtful forms and themes, investigative / innovative / experimental approaches, audience appeal, and how the piece will fit with or complement others we are considering. None of these criteria is a must; none are more important than the others; excellence in any one or two areas may be sufficient for acceptance. Shorts are preferred. Documentaries and animations will be considered. Note that we are not interested in simple recordings of dance on a proscenium stage — cinematic elements must be an integral part of the entry.
  • Exhibition. Accepted submissions will be screened at our primary venue in Boulder, CO, USA and will tour to other venues around the U.S. and elsewhere. The tour schedule will be posted on our web site when specific dates and times become available.
