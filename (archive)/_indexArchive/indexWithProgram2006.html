detailed program
  Sans Souci is an international festival dedicated to projecting moving images of dance through space onto reflective surfaces. The festival started in Boulder Colorado in 2003 with the support of the Boulder Museum of Contemporary Art (BMoCA) and the University of Colorado at Boulder. With an expansive definition of dance and an appreciation for highly experimental and interdisciplinary forms, this unique festival exposes diverse audiences to a variety of film, video, and performance possibilities.

[The first Sans Souci] Festival really opened my eyes to the variety of categories under the heading of dance film and inspired me to create more in this medium. ... I'm so excited for this year's festival!
     Cari Cunningham, Dance MFA, CU Boulder
